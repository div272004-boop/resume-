Who I Am

About & Educational Philosophy

I believe that education should be active, inquiry-based, and student-centered. Social Science is not just about memorizing dates and facts; it is about cultivating global citizenship, historical empathy, and critical geographical thinking in young learners.

My goal is to create inclusive, digital-friendly classrooms where students are encouraged to collaborate, question, and apply their learning to local and global contexts using standard pedagogical structures and frameworks.

"To contribute to students' academic and holistic development by delivering engaging, curriculum-based Social Science instruction and fostering a positive, organized, and learner-centered classroom environment."
